Operation PowerOff

This website has been seized as part of Operation PowerOFF. Operation PowerOFF is an international law enforcement effort focused on shutting down DDoS-for-hire services, including booters and stressers.

DDoS attacks are illegal. For years law enforcement agencies around the world have seized booter databases, arrested administrators, and collected information relating to the operation of these services, including information on the customers of these services. Anyone operating or utilizing DDoS services is subject to investigation, prosecution, and other law enforcement action.
